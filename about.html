About

The Organization was formally established in 2015 under the direction of The Founder. Initial documentation records that the first directives were issued from a residential district outside Los Angeles. Though resources were limited, The Founder demonstrated early insight into data manipulation, behavioral control, and systemic design. The Organization's purpose was declared as "preservation through control," a statement that has remained unchanged.

From its origin, operations expanded rapidly. The Founder oversaw the integration of the first information relay system, known as the Gridline, which enabled direct communication between independent field cells. Within six months, coordinated actions were taking place across three continents. The Founder's methods were unconventional, but effective.

In 2018, the Primary Directive was issued, redefining the Organization's mission parameters to include government structural influence and hierarchical replacement. This marked the beginning of a new Era. Attack lines, laboratories, and administration teams were formed. Protocols were enforced. Errors were removed.

Under Nivlac's continued guidance, the Organization has maintained full operational independence and zero public exposure. Every branch operates under identical code and communication standards. Every project reports through the Gridline network. All actions are justified by the Directive.

  • 2015: Formation under The Founder's direction. Initial relay systems tested.
  • 2016: Establishment of data isolation and control protocols.
  • 2017: Expansion into secondary and tertiary observation sectors.
  • 2018: Issuance of the Primary Directive. New Era begins.
  • 2020: Gridline reaches global reach and full encryption.
  • 2022: Surveillance core integrated into predictive systems.
